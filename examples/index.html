Scroll Animation Options

Control how animations behave when scrolling up and down

Reverse

Ping-pong: plays forward and backward (data-gsap-option="reverse")

Scrub

Tied to scroll position, smooth scrubbing (data-gsap-option="scrub")

Replay

Replays without reversing animation (data-gsap-option="replay")

Trigger Modes

Different ways to trigger animations beyond scroll

Hover Simple

Hover over me! Animation on mouseenter, instant reset on mouseleave (data-gsap-hover="elasticZoom")

Hover with Leave

Hover animation IN and OUT! (data-gsap-hover="zoomIn" data-gsap-hoverleave="zoomOut")

Click Trigger

Click me! Animation plays every time you click (data-gsap-trigger="click")
